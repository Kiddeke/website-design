Why Full Clear exists

Ten years of watching the same pattern.

After 10+ years working at the intersection of finance and IT, one pattern kept showing up: consultants who understood the technology but not the business, or finance leaders who knew exactly what they needed but couldn't translate it into implementation requirements.

Hours burned in meetings just getting both sides on the same page. Requirements lost in translation. Deliverables that technically worked but missed the point.

Full Clear was founded to eliminate that gap. With a finance background and an IT focus, we speak both languages fluently — so nothing gets lost between what your team needs and what gets built.
