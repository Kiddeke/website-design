What We Implement

OneStream replaces fragmented point solutions with a single platform. Here's what we deliver across its core capability areas.

Close & Consolidation

Automate the financial close from data collection through certified consolidation, eliminations, and journal entries.

  • Financial Close management with Task Manager workflows
  • Account Reconciliations with GL trial balance matching
  • Report distribution via Parcel Services

Planning & Forecasting

Build driver-based budgets, rolling forecasts, and workforce plans inside the same platform as your actuals.

  • Budget, forecast, and what-if scenario modeling
  • People Planning for headcount, salary, and compensation
  • Predictive Analytics with time-series statistical models

Analytics & Machine Learning

Layer ML-driven forecasting and variance analysis on top of your financial data for explanations your team can act on.

  • Sensible Machine Learning for driver-based forecast accuracy
  • Quantifiable variance explanations that surface root causes
  • Scenario analysis and model deployment within the platform

Governance & Controls

Ensure audit readiness with built-in approval workflows, metadata governance, and change management controls.

  • Application Control Manager for metadata and privilege governance
  • Multi-level approval workflows with audit trail
  • Data lineage and certified consolidation logic
