What we collect

This site collects personal information only when you choose to submit it through our contact form (name, email, company, country, and project details) or newsletter signup (email address). Browsing the site does not require an account and does not collect personal information.

Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry and scope potential work. Newsletter signups are used to send occasional updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form processing

Forms on this site are processed by Formspree, which transmits your submission to us by email. Formspree's handling of that data is described in the Formspree privacy policy.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party cookies. The only data stored in your browser is your color-theme preference, kept in local storage on your own device and never transmitted to us.

Your choices
